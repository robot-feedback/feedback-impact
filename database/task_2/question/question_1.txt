The fast developement of tecnology has change many aspects of our lifes. We are depend on devices that make living easier and more convinient. However, this reliance also bring problem. Some argues that too much use of smartphones effects our social skills. This is particulary evidence in younger generation who seem more focused on their screen then the real world. Additionally, there are many apps, like weather or news, that provide us useful informations. Yet, we dont always check the sources accuratly. Consistently using unverified informations might lead too misinformation, which is a growing conceren in the modern society, affecting trust and decision-making.
